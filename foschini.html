FOSCHINI

Foschini is the biggest reseller of Adidas and Nike products in South Africa where it is the No.3 clothes retailer by market value and operates more than 2 000 stores. ... Home to some of the world's fastest-growing economies, sub-Saharan Africa is increasingly attracting interest from retail executives across the world.

It currently has about 130 outlets on the continent outside it home market, mostly in southern African countries including Namibia, Lesotho and Botswana. But CEO Doug Murray said the company planned to up that number to around 350 by 2020.
