BONJOUR

Bonjour Supermarket also known as BON SU, is an online shop launched in 2012 for the ones with a sensitive playful eye, and an interest for artist publications, music and contemporary crafts.

While BON SU is Poetic Pastel Press' official online shop since 2015, it also offers publications by imprints including Nidi Gallery Press and Earth.Rope.Pot.Plant, alongside a selection of carefully sourced objects of the daily. For the past years, BON SU has faithfully been commissioning and presenting vessel collections by Laurence Labbe, Olivia Fiddes and Sigrid Volders. BON SU aims to support independent creatives. A project personally curated, and run by French interdisciplinary artist Johanna Tagada.

BON SU is a United-Kingdom operated project offering worldwide shipping. Please note that as a small independant initivate, orders are shipped once a week, usually on Tuesdays.
